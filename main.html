3 Idiots

A Bollywood classic that blends comedy, friendship, and life lessons

3 Idiots tells the story of three engineering students and highlights the importance of following passion over societal pressure.

Actors

The movie stars Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, Sharman Joshi as Raju, and Kareena Kapoor as Pia. Each actor delivers a memorable performance that adds depth and realism to the story.
