Summary

3 Idiots follows the journey of three engineering students and their friendship as they navigate academic pressure, personal dreams, and societal expectations. Through humor and emotional moments, the movie encourages viewers to pursue passion instead of blindly chasing success.

Actors

The movie stars Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, Sharman Joshi as Raju, and Kareena Kapoor as Pia. Each actor delivers a memorable performance that adds depth and realism to the story.
